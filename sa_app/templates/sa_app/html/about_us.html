This is about us page

A Sport Academy is defined as a registered organization that exists to promote and develop interest in a particular sport or physical activity. The Sporting Edge Academy is a web-based platform designed to provide a comprehensive solution for a specific sport academy to manage its sports activities and operations. This web application is aimed at streamlining the processes of registration/ admission, scheduling, payment and communication.
